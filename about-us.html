About OPLEE

WHAT IS OPROMA?

We are a skincare, haircare, and healthcare products manufacturing plant in Sri Lanka. We are a GMP and ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We do contract manufacturing, where the customers can manufacture their own brand and take it as a finished product from us. Other than contract manufacturing, we have our own brand, Oplee Luxury Products. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and follows strict quality control measures to ensure the highest standards are met. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing top-notch services and products to meet our clients' needs.

OUR RITUAL OF WELL-BEING

We believe that skincare can help with all aspects of your physical, mental, and emotional well-being because your skin affects and reflects your overall health. Our OPLEE Luxury skincare products can be used as the foundation for broader well-being rituals that influence the mind and engage the five senses to promote harmony from the skin to the soul.

OUR SKINCARE MYTHOLOGY

Each formula is meticulously developed by Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd in Sri Lanka, the manufacturer of the OPLEE luxury range, where skilled and experienced chemists innovate in keeping with tradition, combining timeless Sri Lankan botanicals with proven clinical ingredients that work with the skin, not against her. Inside every OPLEE formula contains a specific brilliant vibe. This proprietary complex gives out the best cosmetic innovation in terms of quality efficacy and safety.

BIRTH OF OPLEE LUXURY PRODUCTS

It has been observed that many consumers in Sri Lanka prefer purchasing imported products over supporting local businesses, despite the fact that some of these imported products are not authentic. Additionally, consumers often experience confusion about proper skincare routines and solutions. For instance, individuals with acne seek not only to cure their condition but also to achieve healthier skin overall. Many consumers are currently struggling without access to effective products that address their specific skin concerns. While luxury brands are available in the market, they often fail to resolve clients’ issues, providing only the self-satisfaction associated with owning a high-demand, recognizable product. The true solution lies in offering effective products that target specific skin problems and deliver visible results. After nearly five years of dedicated research and development, we have finalized a select number of products. Instead of launching an extensive product line, we have chosen to introduce four highly result-oriented products. Prior to their market release, these products underwent rigorous in-house testing to ensure their effectiveness. They were distributed to close associates for treating their skin issues, and we observed significant improvements. We collected feedback and documented the progress with before-and-after images, which confirmed the efficacy of our products.

WHY OPLEE IS DIFFERENT?

We promise to help you achieve harmony from the skin to the soul. The skin is a wondrous organ that assists you in your life 's journey. We are conscious of how we portray and explain skin, recognizing its vitality as an organ while avoiding unhealthy beauty standards. The term "normal" as a skin type is being phased out because it implies that the natural condition of the skin is abnormal. We are committed to portraying skin as it truly is, celebrating it at all ages and in all shades of beauty. Each formula is meticulously developed by Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd in Sri Lanka, the manufacturer of the OPLEE luxury range. Our skilled and experienced chemists innovate in keeping with tradition, combining timeless Sri Lankan botanicals with proven clinical ingredients that work with the skin, not against it. Every OPLEE formula contains a proprietary complex that embodies the best in cosmetic innovation in terms of quality, efficacy, and safety.

To establish OPLEE Luxury Products as the leading brand in skincare, transforming beauty standards, setting new beauty norms, and promoting well-being through innovative, result-oriented products and empowering messages.

To develop and market a comprehensive range of meticulously crafted OPLEE luxury skincare products that effectively address various skin issues and deliver exceptional results.
