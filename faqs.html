What products do you recommend for oily and acne-prone skin?

For oily and acne-prone skin, we recommend the OPLEE Tea Tree Face Wash and OPLEE Mint Cleanser. These products are designed to control excess oil and help manage acne.

Can people with dry skin use the Tea Tree Face Wash and Mint Cleanser?

No, we advise against using these products if you have dry skin, as they are formulated to control oil, which may further dry out your skin.

What is the best OPLEE product for dry and combination skin?

The OPLEE Skin Glow Fairness Night Cream is ideal for dry and combination skin. It helps with pigmentation, acne marks, sunburn marks, and dull skin. However, it is not suitable for acne-prone skin.

Can I use the Skin Glow Fairness Night Cream if I have acne-prone skin?

No, the Skin Glow Fairness Night Cream is not recommended for acne-prone skin. It is better suited for addressing pigmentation and dullness in dry and combination skin.

What should I use for dry skin?

For dry skin, we recommend the OPLEE Skin Glow Fairness Body Lotion. It promotes healthy, glowing skin and is excellent for dry skin. Please note, it does not offer sun protection, so it should not be used during sunny days.

Can I use the OPLEE Dan Zero Shampoo and OPLEE Loss Zero Shampoo together?

No, it is not advisable to use these medicated shampoos together. Use the OPLEE Dan Zero Shampoo to treat dandruff first, and once the dandruff is under control, switch to the OPLEE Loss Zero Shampoo. For best results, use these shampoos 3-4 times per week.

Where can I purchase OPLEE products?

You can purchase OPLEE products through our social media pages or by contacting our hotlines for more information.

All Oplee products are certified by the National Drug Regulatory Authority. Oproma Cosmetics Pvt Ltd, the manufacturer of Oplee products, is a company that holds both GMP and ISO certifications.
