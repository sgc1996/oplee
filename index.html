OPLEE LUXURY PRODUCTS

OPLEE Luxury Products are designed to harmonize your skin and soul. We celebrate skin’s natural beauty at every age and in all shades, while steering clear of outdated beauty standards. Crafted by Oproma Cosmetics Pvt Ltd which is a GMP and ISO certified company in Sri Lanka, our products blend traditional botanicals with scientifically proven ingredients to ensure superior quality, efficacy, and safety. Plus, all OPLEE products are proudly NMRA (National Medicine Regulatory Authority) certified.

“A NEW LUXURY MILESTONE IN THE SCIENCE OF SKINCARE. UNCOMPROMISING BEAUTY.”

WHY OPLEE IS DIFFERENT

We promise to help you achieve harmony from the skin to the soul. The skin is a wondrous organ that assists you in your life's journey. We are conscious of how we portray and explain skin, recognizing its vitality as an organ while avoiding unhealthy beauty standards. The term "normal" as a skin type is being phased out because it implies that the natural condition of the skin is abnormal. We are committed to portraying skin as it truly is, celebrating it at all ages and in all shades of beauty.


Each formula is meticulously developed by Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd in Sri Lanka, the manufacturer of the OPLEE luxury range. Our skilled and experienced chemists innovate in keeping with tradition, combining timeless Sri Lankan botanicals with proven clinical ingredients that work with the skin, not against it. Every OPLEE formula contains a proprietary complex that embodies the best in cosmetic innovation in terms of quality, efficacy, and safety.
